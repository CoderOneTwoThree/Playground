Bio

Jacob Reynolds is Head of Policy at MCC Brussels. His background spans philosophy studies at Oxford, strategy consulting, and building public debate programs, including the Battle of Ideas festival and The Academy. He is currently a PhD candidate at Vrije Universiteit Brussel and author of Beyond the Culture Wars.
